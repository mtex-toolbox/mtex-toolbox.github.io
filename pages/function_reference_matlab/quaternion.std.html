mean angular deviation

Syntax

{% highlight matlab %} [m, lambda, V] = std(q) [m, lambda, V] = std(q,'robust') [m, lambda, V] = std(q,'weights',weights) {% endhighlight %}

Input

q list of quaternion
weights list of weights

Output

m mean orientation
lambda principle moments of inertia
V principle axes of inertia (quaternion)
