homochoric coordinates of a quaternion

The 3-d homochoric coordinates of a quaternion results from the equal area lambert transformation of the 4-d quaternion coordinates on the unit sphere onto the ball.

Syntax

{% highlight matlab %} v = homochoric(q) {% endhighlight %}

Input

q quaternion

Output

v vector3d
