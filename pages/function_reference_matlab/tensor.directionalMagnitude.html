magnitude of a tensor in direction v

Formula

q = T_i1i2i3...id v_i1 v_i2 v_i3 ... v_id

Syntax

{% highlight matlab %} T = directionalMagnitude(T,v) {% endhighlight %}

Input

T tensor
v direction (vector3d / Miller)

Output

q magnitude of a tensor in direction v
