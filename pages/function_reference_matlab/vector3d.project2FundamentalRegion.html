projects vectors to the fundamental sector of the inverse pole figure

Syntax

{% highlight matlab %} v = project2FundamentalRegion(v,cs) v = project2FundamentalRegion(v,cs,'antipodal') v = project2FundamentalRegion(v,cs,center) {% endhighlight %}

Input

v vector3d
cs symmetry
center vector3d

Output

v vector3d

Options

antipodal include antipodal symmetry
