calculate entropy of SO3Fun

The entropy of a SO3Fun f is defined as:

\[ e = - \int_{SO(3)} f® \ln f® dR\]

Syntax

{% highlight matlab %} e = entropy(SO3F) {% endhighlight %}

Input

SO3F SO3Fun

Output

e double

Options

resolution resolution of the discretization
