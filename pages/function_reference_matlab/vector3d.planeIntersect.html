intersection of three planes

Syntax

{% highlight matlab %} v = planeIntersect(n1,n2,n3,d) {% endhighlight %}

Input

n1,n2,n3 plane normal vector3d, <x,n_i> = d_i
d [d1,d2,d3] distances of the three planes to the origin

Output

v vector3d
